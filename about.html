Howdy! I'm Blake Prescott.

I am a 28 year old web developer based out of Anacortes Washington. I enjoy delving into how people interact with systems by identifying inefficiencies and creating optimizations. This can mean finding the perfect line of play in a game, or writing a better algorithm to solve my problems. I find great enjoyment in learning new skills and honing old ones.


Amongst my internet peers, I am known to be somewhat of a lab rat. In fighting games, the process of discovering and practicing techniques in the practice range is known as "labbing". In that same way, I prefer to make sure that what I create is tested, reviewed, and thoroughly considered before its debut.
